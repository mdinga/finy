About the Founder

Hi, I’m Mbasa Dinga.

I created Finy because I understand what it feels like to carry responsibilities from every direction. Work, projects, home, follow ups and personal goals can quickly become mentally overwhelming.

Finy was built to help professionals move from overload to clarity by creating a trusted system for capturing, organising and acting on responsibilities.

My goal is simple: help people work with more calm, more structure and more control.
